Get help now if this is urgent

  • Signs of a heart attack — chest pain, pressure, heaviness or tightness
  • Signs of a stroke — face drooping, can’t lift both arms, speech difficulty
  • Severe difficulty breathing — choking, gasping, can’t speak
  • Heavy bleeding
  • Seizure (fit) or unconsciousness
  • Sudden swelling of lips, mouth, throat or tongue
  • Signs of sepsis — very unwell, mottled/blue skin, stiff neck, confusion

If none of the above apply, you can continue to request a consultation.
